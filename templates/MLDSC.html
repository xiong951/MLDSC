MLDSC - A machine learning based method for automatic

differential scanning calorimetry signal analysis

MLDSC is a semi-supervised learning based method. The method automates the analysis of DSC signals to extract thermodynamically informative parameters. It can perform baseline estimation, peak identification, and enthalpy integration, peak deconvolution. This method requires DSC data for biomolecular information. Meanwhile, the improved DSC analysis framework accurately separates endothermic and exothermic processes by employing a dual-channel processing algorithm and investigates their thermal properties by integrating independent thermodynamic models.
